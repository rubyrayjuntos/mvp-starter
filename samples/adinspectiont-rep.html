Property Inspection Report

A residential inspection will include the following:

  • Grading
  • Foundation
  • Roofing Interior
  • Exterior
  • HVAC
  • Plumbing
  • Electrical
  • Appliances
  • Insulation
  • Structure

Thermal Imaging of walls and ceilings for possible missing or lacking insulation, electrical and breaker box for possible hot or glowing electrical, also look for possible moisture intrusion.

Also included with every residential inspection is an Interactive 360 Inspection Report with 360 pictures of every room that you can grab the room, spin around, look up and down plus zoom in certain areas of the room. So if you forgot with flooring, paint or tile a room has you can go to the room and look. You can also use this tool to figure out how to set up rooms with furniture.

Residential Inspection report is provided the same day of the inspection.
